Access point in a wireless network is any device that will allow the wireless devices to a wired network. 
A router is the best example of an Access Point.

A Wireless Distribution System allows the connection of multiple access points together. It is used to 
expand a wireless network to a larger network.

Piconet is the basic unit of a bluetooth system having a master node and upto seven active slave nodes. 
A collection of piconets is called scatternet.

Bluetooth is a wireless technology used to create a wireless personal area network for data transfer up 
to a distance of 10 meters. It operates on 2.45 GHz frequency band for transmission.

Unauthorised access of information from a wireless device through a bluetooth connection is called Bluesnarfing.

RTP - Real-time transport protocol and is for delivering audio and video over IP networks. 
Its applications include streaming media, video teleconference, and television services.
